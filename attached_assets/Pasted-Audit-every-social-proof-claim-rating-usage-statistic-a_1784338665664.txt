Audit every social-proof claim, rating, usage statistic, and testimonial displayed on Xuvilo.

Potentially unsupported claims currently include:

- “Trusted by 2,000+ businesses”
- “Thousands of companies and freelancers trust our platform”
- A 4.8/5 rating
- “Join thousands of users”
- Document-created counters
- Named customer testimonials
- Claims that users use Xuvilo daily

Do not assume these claims are genuine.

For every claim:

1. Identify its source in the code or database.
2. Identify the underlying evidence or analytics calculation.
3. Determine whether it represents verified real users, registered accounts, generated documents, businesses, sessions, or placeholder marketing text.
4. Do not count test accounts, seed data, bots, internal usage, or fabricated examples.
5. Check whether testimonial names, job titles, countries, and quotations belong to real customers who consented to publication.

Required action:

- Retain a numerical claim only if it can be calculated from reliable production data.
- Clearly label the basis of ratings, such as the platform from which the rating originates.
- Remove ratings that have no verifiable source.
- Remove fictional testimonials or replace that section with factual product benefits.
- Never present sample personas as real customers.
- If real testimonials exist, store and display only approved testimonials with documented consent.
- Ensure structured data does not contain fabricated AggregateRating, Review, user-count, or testimonial information.
- Ensure Arabic and English versions use the same verified facts.

Do not expose personal customer information while completing this audit.

Do not report completion until you provide:

- An inventory of every trust claim found.
- The evidence source for each retained claim.
- A list of every removed or rewritten claim.
- Structured-data validation results.
- Modified files and database records.
- Production-build results.
- Live production verification.

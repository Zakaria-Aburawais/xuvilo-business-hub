## CONTEXT
Xuvilo Business Hub is a bilingual (Arabic/English) business document generator targeting freelancers and small businesses in the MENA region. It already has: Invoice Generator, Quotation Generator, Receipt Generator, Document Templates, Stamp Maker, Business Card Maker, Company Profile, Temp Email, Document Tracker, VAT Calculator, Currency Exchange. It is ZATCA Phase 1 compliant and supports 176+ currencies with Arabic RTL native support.

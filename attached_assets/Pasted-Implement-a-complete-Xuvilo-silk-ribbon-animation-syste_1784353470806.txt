2. The individual blue, purple, and cyan ribbon pieces gently separate from the symbol.
3. Each ribbon moves independently around the loading screen.
4. Each piece follows a different smooth, curved flight path.
5. The ribbons must look like flexible silk strips floating in a gentle current.
6. Use subtle bending, rotation, tilt, perspective, and changes in apparent width.
7. Do not use only straight diagonal movement.
8. Keep every ribbon fully inside the visible viewport.
9. Do not let the movement appear chaotic or like confetti.
10. After moving around the screen, all ribbons return smoothly to the centre.
11. They reconnect precisely and rebuild the original Xuvilo symbol.
12. When the symbol is complete, add a brief, subtle branded glow or pulse.
13. Display the complete “Xuvilo Business Hub” wordmark after the ribbons reconnect.
14. If the page is still loading, repeat the separate–fly–return–reassemble sequence naturally.
15. When essential content is ready, complete the current return movement where reasonably possible, show the assembled logo briefly, and fade smoothly into the website.

The complete loading-animation cycle should last approximately 2.5–4 seconds.

Loading behavior requirements:

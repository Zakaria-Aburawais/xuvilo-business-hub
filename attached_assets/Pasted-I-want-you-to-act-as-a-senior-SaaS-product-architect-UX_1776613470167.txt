## Project name
Businesses Hub

## Project concept
Businesses Hub is a bilingual web platform in Arabic and English that offers practical business tools for freelancers, contractors, traders, import/export businesses, and small companies.

The platform should start as a tools-based website, but be structured so it can later grow into a lightweight SaaS platform.

## Core idea
The website will include:
1. Invoice Generator
2. Quotation Generator
3. Receipt Generator
4. Business Calculators
5. PDF export / print-ready documents

## Product goal
Help users do common business tasks quickly:
- create invoices
- create quotations
- create receipts
- calculate important financial and operational numbers
- export clean PDFs
- use the platform in Arabic and English
- later save documents and use dashboards

## Target users
- freelancers
- contractors
- traders
- small business owners
- import/export businesses
- Arabic-speaking SMEs
- English-speaking SMEs in the same markets
